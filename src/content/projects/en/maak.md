Maak, a comprehensive telemedicine app designed to prioritize your health and well-being. At its core, Maak focuses on patient care and mental health, ensuring you receive the support you need *as integrated care. Beyond this, Maak offers a range of services including family medicine consultations, psychiatrist and psychologist consultations , and prescription refills. With Maak, booking an appointment is effortless—whether you need an instant consultation or prefer to schedule one at your convenience, you can start your medical journey immediately. Experience seamless healthcare with Maak, where your health is always a priority.
