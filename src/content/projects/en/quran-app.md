## Learn the Qur’an easily

In the app, you can read and listen to the Qur’an in different modes: word-by-word, with Tajweed rules, and with translation.

## Choose reading modes

There are several modes to practice reading in Arabic. Read word-by-word, with Tajweed and waqf rules, and improve your recitation.

## Understand with translation

View the verses in both languages together. You can read, listen, and repeat.
