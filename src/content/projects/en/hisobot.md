Hisobot.com: your all-in-one business management solution.

Transform the way you run your business with the official Hisobot.com mobile app. Designed to streamline your workflow and improve teamwork, Hisobot.com puts all the powerful features of the platform right at your fingertips. Whether you are at the office or on the go, stay connected and in control of your business operations.

## Key features

- **Advanced task management.** Get a clear view of all your projects and tasks. With an intuitive dashboard you can view, manage and track tasks in a flexible table format. Easily monitor progress, set priorities and keep deadlines on track.
- **Integrated calendar.** Organize your schedule and view tasks by day, week or month with a powerful calendar system. Plan your workload efficiently and never miss an important deadline again.
- **Company structure and employee management.** Set up your company structure by creating departments and managing employee profiles. Keep track of your team, assign roles and streamline communication across the whole organization.
- **Seamless Telegram integration.** Use the Telegram bot to register new employees and manage tasks right from the Telegram app. Get instant notifications and stay up to date on the go.
- **Better teamwork.** Build a collaborative environment with tools designed for working together. Share files, chat with teammates and work on projects side by side to reach your business goals.
