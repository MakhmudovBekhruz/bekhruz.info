IZI study is a modern language-learning app. Learning foreign languages is now easy, convenient and effective.

With IZI study you can learn:

- Russian
- English
- German
- French
- Arabic

We know that getting started can be hard. That is why every user gets 3 free lessons — try the course, evaluate the format and see for yourself how easy and fun learning a language with IZI study can be.

## Why IZI study?

- Simple, clear lessons — new words and rules stick quickly and easily.
- Convenient interface — all lessons, exercises and results at your fingertips.
- Modern methodology — knowledge is reinforced through associations, flashcards and repetition.
- For every level — for beginners and continuing learners alike.
- Learn anywhere — at home, at work or on the road. All you need is your phone.

## What you get

- new vocabulary;
- a grasp of grammar through simple examples;
- reading, writing, listening and speaking skills;
- the confidence to communicate in a foreign language.

## Our approach

We believe a language is a key to new opportunities. Learning with IZI study is not dry theory but a lively, engaging journey.

## Start for free

Download the app, get 3 free lessons, pick a language and start learning today!

IZI study — an easy path to new knowledge and opportunities.
